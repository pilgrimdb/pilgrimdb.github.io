The Association Les Chemins de Mont Saint-Michel, together with the Association Normande des Amis de Saint Jacques, have created a network of routes to Mont St-Michel: from Barfleur and Cherbourg (les Chemins aux Anglais - 160km), from Caen (150km to Les Genêts) and Rouen, from Paris and Chartres. Guides Les Chemins aux Anglais, Les Chemins des Ducs de Normandie (Caen/Rouen), and Itinéraires de pèlerins de l'Orne au Mont-Saint-Michel (Paris/Chartres) are published by Corlet Edns. A further route from Le Mans via Mayenne has been developed by the Association Sarthoise des Amis de Saint Jacques; see their site for details.
See also the abbey site and Manche Randonnée.
