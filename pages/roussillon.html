The Roussillon Amis de St-Jacques have developed a route linking Narbonne and Perpignan with the Spanish border at the Pyrenean pass at Panissars (near the modern Pertús/Perthus pass); a guide can be downloaded from their website, and there is also a printed guide in French, published by Rando Editions..
