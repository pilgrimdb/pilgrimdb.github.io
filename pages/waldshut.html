A spur from the Neckar-Baar-Jakobusweg at Hüfingen runs to Waldshut and Basel. From Waldshut the route is included in the guide Der Jakobsweg vom Hochrhein bis Vézelay by Berthold Burkhardt and Hans-Jörg Bahmüller. See also Wolfgang Meyer's site for an overview description.
