A 300km route via Hameln/Hamlin linking the two Cistercian monasteries of Loccum, W of Hannover, and Volkenroda, near Muhlhausen, was opened in 2005. A guidebook is available.
The 80km Pilgerweg Mariensee-Loccum consists of a circular route, north and south of the Steinhuder Meer, connecting Loccum with the monastery at Mariensee; see brochure from Tourismusregion Hannover.
