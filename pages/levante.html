A route following the old road from Valencia to Toledo, created by the Amigos del Camino de Santiago: Comunidad Valenciana, who have also produced a guide. The route goes SW from Valencia along the Via Augusta, and then NW via Albacete to Toledo. From there it goes N to Avila, Medina del Campo, and joins the Via de la Plata at Zamora. See Mundicamino site for more detailed description.
The route is largely coincident with the Camino del Sureste.
