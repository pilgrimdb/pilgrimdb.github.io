A route connecting the Pfälzer Jakobsweg via Saarbrücken or Sarreguemines with St Avold and the Trier route at Metz is now complete. Overview descriptions are available on the site of the St. Jakobus-Gesellschaft Rheinland-Pfalz-Saarland. A guide Jakobswege an Saar, Blies und Mosel, by Paul Burgard and Ludwig Linsmayer, is published by the Saarland Landesarchiv, and a brochure Im Zeichen der Muschel is available. The guide to the Pfälzer Jakobsweg also covers the route.
