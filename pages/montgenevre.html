The Amis de St Jacques Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur-Corse and the FFRP have created a route, roughly following the Via Domitia, from the Italian border at Montgenèvre (1850m) and Briançon to Arles. There is a FFRP topoguide, and Haute Provence produce a downloadable brochure on the route in their area.
A link from the Col de Larche (1991m) to this route at St-Geniez, via Barcelonnette and Seyne, is also under development.
See Via Francigena page for link to/from Vercelli and Rome.
