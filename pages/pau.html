An alternative to the Camino Aragones and the Somport, the old route over the Col de Pau/Puerto del Palo to the Carolingian monastery of Siresa, which some claim as the original Roman road, is marked on the Spanish side as the GR653-3. After Siresa, the GR does not descend the Aragon valley to Puente la Reina de Jaca but goes further west, rejoining the Aragones at Berdun or Martes.
See vppyr.free.fr or peregrinos.wordpress.com.
