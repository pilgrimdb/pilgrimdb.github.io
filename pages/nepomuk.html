Existing long-distance paths have been adapted to connect Prague with the Ostbayerischer Jakobsweg at Eschlkam via Přibram and Nepomuk. The Fränkische St. Jakobusgesellschaft Würzburg site describes the recommended route; it is also described in the Tyrolia guide to the Ostbayerischer Jakobsweg
