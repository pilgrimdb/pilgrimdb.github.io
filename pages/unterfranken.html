leads from the Fuldaer Land Jakobsweg at Fulda via the Kreuzberg to Würzburg. A guide Der Jakobsweg von Fulda nach Würzburg, by Rudolf Beck, is published by Michael Imhof Verlag. A detailed description of the 110km route from Fulda to Schweinfurt is on the Fränkische St. Jakobus-Gesellschaft Würzburg site, as is an accommodation list Fulda-Würzburg. Another guide to the 56km route from Schweinfurt to Würzburg Der Unterfränkische Jakobusweg, by Roland Breitenbach, is published by Maier of Schweinfurt.
