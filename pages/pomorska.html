A coastal route which is planned to eventually run from the Lithuanian town of Klaipeda via Russian Kaliningrad and Gdansk to the Via Baltica at Świnoujście/Swinemünde is under development. The section from Sianówo to Smołdzino, aka the Lęborska Droga św. Jakuba, is now open.
