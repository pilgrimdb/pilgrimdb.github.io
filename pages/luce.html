The Cammino della Luce is a long-term plan to develop a route, also known as the Itinerario della Via Romea del Corridoio Bizantino e della Via Amerina, connecting Aquileia with the Byzantine territories around Ravenna, and the Via Amerina via Assisi, Todi and Orte to Rome.
