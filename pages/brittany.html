routes through Brittany from Pointe-St-Mathieu, Locquirec (plus spur from Moguériec and St Pol de Léon), Abbaye de Beauport have been developed by the Breton Amis de St Jacques. These meet at Redon and continue via Nantes to Clisson, where the Association Vendéenne have developed a continuation, joining the Tours route at St Jean d'Angély (Vendée section 150km; continuation to St Jean 60km). A guide to the routes as far as Clisson Chemins de Saint-Jacques en Bretagne has been published by Rando Editions. A guide to the Clisson-St Jean section is available and can be downloaded from the Association's website.
See also the routes from Mt-St-Michel via Rennes and Angers.
