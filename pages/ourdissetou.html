from the Chemin du Piémont at Lortet W of St-Bertrand, the GR105 has been marked via the Hospice de Rioumajou and the Port d'Ourdissetou/Puerto de Urdiceto to Gistain in Aragon. See site of the Hautes-Pyrenées Amis, and overview on the Hautes-Pyrenées FFRP site. See also vppry.free.fr.
From Gistain the GR19, GR15 and a PR can be followed W to the Camino Aragonés near Jaca. Or see Voie du Piémont Sud Pyrénéen for a suggested route to Sabiñánigo on the Alto Gállego route based on these GRs.
