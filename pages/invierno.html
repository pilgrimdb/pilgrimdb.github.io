From Ponferrada, an alternative lower-level route to the Camino Francés called the Camino Sur or Camino de Invierno has been developed. This leaves the Camino Francés at Ponferrada and runs via Monforte de Lemos and Chantada, joining the Camino Sanabrés near Lalín. A guide in Castilian/Galician is available, with an English/French version planned. There is a guide to the route at Mundicamino. An online description is posted on the Peregrinos a Santiago forum. See Asociación Camiños a Santiago pola Ribeira Sacra site for a detailed description of the 105km section in the Province of Lugo; there is also an overview of this in the Diputación de Lugo website. And see also Valdeorras Amigos site.
The CSJ now have an online guide to this route.
This route is an official Xunta-supported route, but to date no work has been done on it.
