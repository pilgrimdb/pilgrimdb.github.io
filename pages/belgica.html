The Walloon Association des Amis de St Jacques de Compostelle together with the Walloon GR organisation have created this route linking Hélécine south of Tienen with the GR655 at St Quentin via Nivelles and Maubeuge. A guidebook is available. In France, this is marked as the GR655.
