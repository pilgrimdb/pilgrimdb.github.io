Waymarked as the GR653, though it differs from the Codex Calixtinus route as it does not go to St Thibéry. For the French side, see the website of the Amis de St Jacques groups in S France, who are also working on extensions from Menton and Montgenèvre/Briancon. See also Languedoc-Roussillon Amis, and overview information on viatolosana.free.fr and chemindarles.free.fr; the latter includes a large photo gallery. The City of Montpellier has waymarked a route Lo Camin Romieu through the city centre.
At the Somport it connects to the Camino Aragonés
