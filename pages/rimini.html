The CAI Rimini have created this route, firstly along the crest via Sasso di Simone to Sansepolcro, then to Magione on Lago Trasimeno, and finally to Orvieto. The plan is that it will continue to the Via Francigena at Viterbo, and back to Venice and Aquileia.
Maps are available from the Istituto Geografico Adriatico.
