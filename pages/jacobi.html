The E-W routes across Switzerland have recently been revamped.
There are now 4 routes from Austria and SW Germany which converge on Einsiedeln:
  • the Schwabenweg from Konstanz
  • the Appenzellerweg from the Austrian border and Appenzell
  • the St Gallerweg from Rorschach and St Gallen
  • the Thurgauer Klosterweg from Schaffhausen

From Einsiedeln there are two routes W across central Switzerland to Lausanne and the GR65 near Geneva: a northern route via Lucerne and Bern, and a southern one via Interlaken. For details of the route, see jakobsweg.ch website. A guidebook in German, Jakobswege durch die Schweiz, and in French, Chemins de Saint-Jacques à travers la Suisse, by Jolanda Blum is published by Ottverlag of Thun.
Detailed mapping overlaid on Swiss topo maps is available on the wanderland.ch site.
