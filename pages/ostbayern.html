from the Czech/German border at Vseruby/Eschlkam near Furth im Wald, the Fränkische St. Jakobusgesellschaft Würzburg has created the Ostbayerischer Jakobsweg via Regensburg, Kelheim and Eichstätt to the Bayerisch-Schwäbischer Jakobusweg at Donauwörth. A guide by Hans J. Kolbinger is published by Tyrolia.
