This is waymarked as the GR655 from the Via Gallia Belgica at St Quentin via Noyon and Compiègne to Paris.
Pending publication of a full FFRP topoguide, the Picardy region of the FFRP have produced an interim guide to their section to Viarmes. There is an overview of the route in the Ile de France on the regional FFRP site.

As the GR meanders considerably in places, the Amis de Saint Jacques à Compiègne publish a more direct line in two sections, St Quentin-Compiègne (70km) and Compiègne-Paris (93km), collectively dubbed the Chemin Estelle.
