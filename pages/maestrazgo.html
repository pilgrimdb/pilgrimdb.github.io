The Amigos in Alcañiz have created the Camino del Maestrazgo Bajo Aragón, a route which runs from Valencia and Castellón via Morella, Monroyo and Alcañiz to the Ebro route at Escatrón.
In its southern section, this is also known as the Camino de la Plana.
