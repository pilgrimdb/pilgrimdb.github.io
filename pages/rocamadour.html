Two routes link the Limoges route with Rocamadour.
  • the Voie de Rocamadour en Limousin et Haut-Quercy links from Bénévent l'Abbaye; a guide is published by Rando Edns
  • the Amis de Saint-Jacques du Limousin-Périgord describe a route from St Léonard de Noblat in their Guide du pèlerin de Saint-Jacques en Limousin-Périgord
See Via Arvernia for routes linking south and east from Rocamadour.
