If any route is The Camí de Sant Jaume, it is this one, created by the Sabadell Amics, and running from Montserrat via Lleida/Lérida to the Ruta del Ebro at Pina de Ebro (250km). Besides the guidebook available from the Sabadell Amics, there is a detailed guide on the Mundicamino site. In the summer of 2008, the Catalan government upgraded the marking and produced their own guidebook. There are also downloadable leaflets on the government website.
