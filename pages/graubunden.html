A E-W route through Graubünden from the Italian border at Müstair to Amsteg in Uri is now open. A guidebook, Via Son Giachen - Jakobsweg in Graubünden, is available.
Detailed mapping overlaid on Swiss topo maps is available on the wanderland.ch site.
The route can be connected with the main ViaJacobi on Lake Lucerne via the ViaGottardo.
