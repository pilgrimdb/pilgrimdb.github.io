A route through Westfalen from Osnabrück via Münster and Dortmund to the Cologne-Aachen route at Wuppertal has been developed by the Landschaftsverband Westfalen-Lippe. A guide, Jakobswege, Wege der Jakobspilger in Westfalen is published by Bachem.
