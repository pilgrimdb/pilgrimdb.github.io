For the section of the GR655 from Paris (see St Quentin route for continuation from north), there is a choice of a western route via Chartres (GR655 ouest) or an eastern route via Orléans (GR655 est).
As the GR meanders considerably, local groups of Amis de St Jacques suggest a more direct route:
  • Etampes-Orleans-Blois, see Loiret group
  • Chaumont-Tours, see Tours group
  • Epernon-Châteaudun, see Chartres group
There is an overview of the route in the Ile de France on the regional FFRP site. The GR route through Eure-et-Loir is shown on the Carte et Guide touristique. The Amis de St Jacques in Tours have a guide to the section from Château-Renault to Tours on their website.
See also Lepère Edns guides for alternative suggested routes to both Paris-Orleans-Tours and Paris-Chartres-Tours.
