The Association Rhône-Alpes des Amis de St-Jacques has marked a spur from Lyon to the Cluny-Le Puy route; this route is also in the Cluny guide.
They have also marked a further spur from Nantua and Ambérieu to Lyon, and also a linked route from L'Arbresle; guides are available on their website.
