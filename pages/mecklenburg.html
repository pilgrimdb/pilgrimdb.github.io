A route connecting Rostock on the Baltisch-Westfälischer Jakobsweg, via Kloster Tempzin and Bad Wilsnack, with Tangermünde on the Sachsen-Anhalt route.
There is a guide to the route as far as Perleberg on the website of the St. Jakobus-Gesellschaft Norddeutschland, and one to the route from Wilsnack to Tangermünde on the St Jakobusgesellschaft Berlin-Brandenburg website. The latter is also described in Brandenburg: Auf den Spuren des mittelalterlichen Pilgerwegs Berlin-Wilsnack-Tangermünde by Rainer Oefelein.
