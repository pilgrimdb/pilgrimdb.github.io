along the north coast from the French border at Irún via Bilbao, Santander and Gijón to Ribadeo and then via Vilalba to Arzúa on the Camino Francés. See overview on Eric Walker's site. The Basque Country tourist board site has a full description for the route in its area, the Guipuzcoa Amigos have a detailed description of their section on their website, the Rincón Astur website has a detailed description of the route in Asturias (the Villaviciosa-Avilés section is described via Oviedo not Gijón), and the Galician Amigos have a detailed description on their website. For a detailed guide on the whole route (also via Oviedo), see Mundicamino.
