A route through the Ardennes, also called the Chemin des Pèlerins or Via Arduinna is under development, from the Via Mosana at Liège to the GR654 at Vitry-le-François. The short section through the Semois, from Suxy to the border near Orval, was opened in 2005; a brochure is available from Semois Tourisme.
For more info on the current status, see Luc Selleslagh's website.
