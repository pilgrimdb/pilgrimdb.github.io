The Cammino di Sant'Antonio was originally a short route linking Camposampiero with Padova. This is now called the Ultimo Cammino, and there is now also the Lungo Cammino, which links Venice with Padova and Padova with the Cammino di Assisi at Montepaolo. The Venice-Padova-Monselice section is largely the same as the Via Romea Leona and the Padova-Ferrara section is largely the same as the Via Romea Stadensis.
