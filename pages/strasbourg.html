A route from the German border at Schweigen/Wissembourg via Strasbourg, Belfort and Cîteaux to Cluny is now complete. There are German spurs from the Pfälzer Jakobsweg to Wissembourg, and from the Kinzigtäler Jakobusweg at Kehl to Strasbourg. See also links from Breisach and Basel. There is also a spur to Vézelay.
See websites of Amis de St Jacques in Alsace, Franche-Comté, and Burgundy for more details.
See the Alsace site for guidebooks in French for Wissembourg-Cluny, and in German for Wissembourg-Belfort and the spurs from Germany. See also the site of the Jakobsweg-Team Winnenden for detailed maps of the route. The Burgundy Amis publish a guide to their route, and there is a description of the connection from Neustadt an der Weinstrasse to Wissembourg on the site of the St. Jakobus-Gesellschaft Rheinland-Pfalz-Saarland.
