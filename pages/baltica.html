A route along the German coast from the Polish/German border on the island of Usedom via Rostock, Lübeck, Hamburg and Bremen to the Westfälischer Jakobsweg at Osnabrück has been developed by the St. Jakobus-Gesellschaft Norddeutschland. See Birgittaweg for the spur from Sassnitz and Stralsund.
A guide to the route from Bremen, Jakobswege: Wege der Jakobspilger in Bremen und Niedersachsen is published by Bachem.
