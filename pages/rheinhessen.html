A route linking the Rhein at Bingen with Worms and the Pfälzer Jakobsweg has been developed by the Sankt Jakobusgesellschaft Rheinland-Pfalz-Saarland. There is a brochure with an overview map on Pilgertouren.de.
The guide to the Pfälzer Jakobsweg also covers the route from Dittelsheim and Worms to Speyer and Standenbühl. See Alsace route for details of connection from Speyer to Schweigen/Wissembourg.
