a route along the river Ebro from Tortosa via the important Jacobean shrine at Zaragoza to Logroño has been created; for details see Mundicamino website. Further details on the section in Navarra on Rutas Navarra and Ribera Amigos site; on the La Rioja section, on Juan Carlos Miguel's site.
This route along the Ebro is supposedly that used by St James when leaving Spain, commemorated by the town in the Ebro delta called Sant Jaume d'Enveja.
