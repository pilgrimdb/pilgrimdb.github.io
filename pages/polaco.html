Camino Polaco is the name originally given to the main E-W route across Poland, from the Lithuanian/Polish border at Ogrodniki to the German border at Frankfurt-an-der-Oder. It now refers to the section as far as Mogilno/Trzemeszno and Gniezno. The section connecting Mogilno with the Wielkopolska Droga at Gniezno is called the Szlak Piastowski. See Lubuska Droga for the final section to the German border..
The route is outlined on the Polish Club of Camino de Santiago site, which gives details of the 240km Olsztyn-Toruń section, opened in 2007, for which a guide is available, and of the link from Toruń to the Szlak Piastowski.
