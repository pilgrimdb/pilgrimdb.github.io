Online guides to the route from Cadiz to Seville are available on the website of the Asociación Gaditana del Camino de Santiago “Vía Augusta”, and on Mundicamino. There is also a printed guide available from the Seville Amigos.
There is another group in Cadiz Asociación de Amigos del Camino de Santiago en Cádiz, though there is no route info on their site at present.
