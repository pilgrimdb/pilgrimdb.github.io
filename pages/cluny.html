The Association Rhône-Alpes des Amis de St-Jacques has waymarked a route from Cluny to Le Puy and published a guide; their website includes information on accommodation. A guide is also published by Chamina.
The Amis have also marked a spur from the TGV station at Mâcon-Loché.
