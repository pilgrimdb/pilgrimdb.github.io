This route connects the St Olav routes in Norway and the St Bridget routes in Sweden through Jutland and Schleswig-Holstein with the Via Baltica near Stade W of Hamburg.

See Foreningen af Danske Santiagopilgrimme site for map of Frederikshavn-Viborg section. From Viborg to the Danish/German border near Flensburg, the route uses the Haervej. For the 180km German section, see via-jutlandica.de and St. Jakobus-Gesellschaft Norddeutschland.
A 210km variant linking Schleswig via Kiel with Lübeck is also now complete.
