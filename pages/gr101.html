For anyone on the Toulouse-Somport route wanting an alternative via Lourdes, the GR101 provides one from Maubourguet, connecting with the Chemin du Piémont, which runs E-W through Lourdes.
For more info on walking routes to/from Lourdes, see separate page based on Google Maps.
