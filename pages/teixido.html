The Club Montaña Ferrol have marked a route from Ferrol to the St Andrew shrine at San Andrés/Santo André de Teixido; map on Valdoviño site. The traditional ways are from Cedeira and Ortigueira.
An extension from the Ermida de Sta Comba to Ferrol was opened in 2008.
