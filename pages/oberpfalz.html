from Czech/German border at Tillyschanz near Vohenstrauss to Nuremberg. There's a description of the 90km route from Tillyschanz to Ensdorf on the Oberpfälzer Waldverein site. The Fränkische Albverein produce a guide to the continuation to Stein bei Nürnberg. There's a 'virtual pilgrim path' for these sections on Hans-Jörg Bahmüller's site, and a guide by Ulrich Beigel is also available.
