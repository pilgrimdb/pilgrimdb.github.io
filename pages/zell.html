The St. Jakobus-Gesellschaft Rheinland-Pfalz-Saarland have developed a route connecting Worms with the Nordpfälzer Jakobsweg at Landstuhl. The guide to the Pfälzer Jakobswege covers the route as far as Standenbühl.
