created by the Jakobuspilgergemeinschaft Augsburg, leading from Oettingen (on the Nuremberg-Ulm route between Gunzenhausen and Nördlingen) via Donauwörth and Augsburg to the Munich route at Buchenberg near Kempten. Between Augsburg and Buchenberg there are 2 variants: one via Bad Wörishofen and Ottobeuren; the other via Babenhausen and Memmingen. There is a guide by Ulrich Lohrmann.
A brochure on the entire route Oettingen-Lake Constance is available from the local tourist office; it can be downloaded as a pdf.
