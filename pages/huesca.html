A route linking Montserrat with San Juan de la Peña, created by the Associació D'Amics dels Pelegrins in Barcelona. It follows the Camí de Sant Jaume as far as Tàrrega, and then heads via Huesca to the Camino Aragonés at San Juan.
There is a detailed guide to the route on the Mundicamino site, and the Oscense Amigos site also has some description and mapping.
