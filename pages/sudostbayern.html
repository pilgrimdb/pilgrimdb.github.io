two routes constituting the Südostbayerischer Jakobsweg are now complete:
  1. from the Austrian route at Salzburg to the Munich route at Hohenpeissenberg (271km)
  2. from Cesky Krumlov in S Bohemia, via the NW corner of Austria to Passau, then on to the Salzburg route near Bad Aibling and the Austrian route at Breitenbach near Kufstein (476km)
A guide Auf dem Jakobsweg durch Südostbayern by Maximilian Bogner is available; see also Der bayerische Jakobsweg by Fritz Fenzl.
There is further info on the Salzburg route and the Krumlov route from Altötting onwards on the Südost-Oberbayern Jakobsweg site.
