A route from Alicante, largely the same as the Camino de Levante, which it joins at Albacete. From Medina del Campo it heads NW to the Via de la Plata at Benavente.
The Alicante Amigos site includes a complete downloadable guide, and there is an online guide on Mundicamino. There is a detailed description for the route in Avila province on the Avila Amigos site, and a description of the route in Valladolid province on the Valladolid Amigos site. The latter also includes a link to the Camino Sanabrés at Sta Marta de Tera.
