A route from the German/Luxembourg border via Metz and Toul to Clairvaux and the GR654 near Les Riceys is under development by the Lorraine Amis.
In the meantime, Conrad Stein publish a guide by Norbert Rother, and there is a description of this route on fernwege.de. This is the origin of the detailed mapping on this site.
Paulinus publish a guide, Wege der Jakobspilger, Bd. 2 - Rheinland, Eifel, Lothrigen, Burgund, by Walter Töpner to another route from Metz via Verdun, Châlons-en-Champagne and Troyes.
