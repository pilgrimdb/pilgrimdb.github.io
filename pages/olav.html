A network of routes leading to the shrine of St Olav in Nidaros/Trondheim has been developed, the main one being that from Oslo. For further information, see site of Norwegian Confraternity of St James and pilegrim.info.
For routes in Sweden see Pilgrim i Sverige, and see pilgrimstid.nu for information on further routes being developed from Hudiksvall and Gävle on the Swedish coast.
