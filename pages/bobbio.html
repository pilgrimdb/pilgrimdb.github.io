This alternative to the Via Francigena via Bobbio itself has various alternatives:
  • the main route is the Via degli Abati, which originally ran between Bobbio and Pontremoli; but has now been extended to/from Pavia. There are 2 guides to the whole route, published by Terre di Mezzo and Ancora Libri; there is an ebook for the latter. For the original route see also official site. There is also a summary in English on Ciao Lunigiana. The Pavia-Bobbio section is also known as the Via di San Colombano.
