A coastal alternative to the Tours route through the Landes, from the Pointe de Grave at the mouth of the Gironde to the Camino de la Costa at Irún. A leaflet for the section through Gironde La Voie de Soulac is available from the Conseil Général. There's a detailed guide of the Landes section on the Landes Amis de St Jacques website. See VTT Compostelle site for a guide to the complete route with detailed mapping.
