A route from Paderborn to the Heidenstrasse near Schmallenberg is being developed by the Freundes­kreis der Jakobus­pilger Pader­born. A provisional leaflet can be downloaded from their website.
An alternative, partly coincident route is provided by the guide, Von Paderborn durch das Sauerland nach Köln, by Heinrich Wipper.
