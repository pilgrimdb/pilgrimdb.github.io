--- title: Caminos del Norte - routes north of Camino Francés id: norte permalink: /norte.html ---
a generic term for the routes to the north of the Camino Francés, and which predate it; the term is sometimes used for the Camino de la Costa and sometimes for a combination of the Camino de la Costa and the Camino Primitivo. Marking is not yet complete on all these routes. See overview map on Eric Walker's site.
See Camino de la Costa; Ruta del Túnel; Camino Primitivo; Ruta Vadiniense; Camino de Besaya; Ruta del Salvador.
