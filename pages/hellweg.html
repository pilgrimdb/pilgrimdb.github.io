A route based on the Hellweg from the Elbe at Magdeburg to the Westfälischer Jakobsweg at Dortmund is being developed. See Altertumskommission für Westfalen for info on Westfalian section from Corvey/Höxter to Dortmund via Soest and Paderborn. A guide is available. See other page for continuation to Neuss and Aachen.
A guide to a route from Magdeburg via Goslar and Höxter to Bonn by Walter Töpner Wege der Jakobspilger has been published by Paulinus.
