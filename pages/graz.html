Peter Lindenthal has written Auf dem Jakobsweg durch Süd-Österreich, Slowenien und Südtirol describing a route through parts of S Austria, Slovenia and N Italy from Graz through Maribor/Marburg and the Pustertal and over the Brenner to join the route from Vienna at Innsbruck. This route is partly waymarked. See Dagmar Steiner's site for info on the Carinthia section, and Jakobsweg Südtirol site for details on Südtirol section. See Tirol Kunstkataster for detailed mapping for the Osttirol and Tirol sections.
