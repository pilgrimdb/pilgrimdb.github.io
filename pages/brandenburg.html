A joint Polish-German development. On the German side, two routes from the Polish border at Frankfurt-an-der-Oder have been opened: via Fürstenwalde to Berlin/Köpenick, and via Strausberg to Bernau. The latter should eventually be extended to the Berlin-Wilsnack route.
A guide, Auf dem Jakobsweg in Brandenburg: Von der Oder bis nach Berlin, is available. See Viadrina website for details.
For the Polish side, see Lubuska Droga. For the connection to the Sachsen-Anhalt route, see Baltisch-Mitteldeutscher Jakobsweg.
