The medieval pilgrimage route from Berlin to the Wunderblut shrine at Bad Wilsnack has been revived. A guide by Rainer Oefelein Brandenburg: Auf den Spuren des mittelalterlichen Pilgerwegs Berlin-Wilsnack-Tangermünde is published by Conrad Stein.
